Frequently Asked Questions

How Long Do the Seasons Last?

  • Spring: March 19th - June 20th
  • Summer: June 20th - September 22nd
  • Fall: September 23rd - December 21st
  • Winter: December 21st - March 19th

    • What is the Difference Between a Solstice and an Equinox?

    A solstice happens on the first day of winter or summer. This happens when the Sun is at its furthest possible point from the equator (winter is far north and summer is farther south). An equinox happens on the first day of Spring and Fall. This is when the Sun is aligned with the equator.
