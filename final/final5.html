No. 2 Kansas Runs Past No. 20 Colorado, 72-58, Behind Stellar Performance From Ochai Agbaji

Udoka Azubuike owns the paint late in the second half and for the whole duration of overtime, with no answer from the Flyers, to crown the Jayhawks as the 2019 Maui Jim Maui Inviational Champions.

Jayhawks Utilize Paint in Struggling Night From Deep to Pull Away From Talented East Tennessee State Squad, 75-63

Kansas uses their advantage in both size and athleticism to own the key on disappointing 1/12 night from three-point range, to defeat a flustered Buccaneers group.
